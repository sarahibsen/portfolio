Chocolate Mug Brownie

Careful not to overcook! Full disclosure, I got this recipe from someone else and you can view their page by clicking the link below.

Original Recipe

This makes a delicious brownie for one in about a minute. I used a 1500 watt microwave, so times may vary based on your wattage. For me, 60 seconds made a molten center brownie, and 75 seconds made a uniformly done brownie.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 dash salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. In a 12 oz coffee mug, add water, butter, vanilla and dash of salt. Whisk well.
  2. Add cocoa powder, whisk well. Add sugar, whisk well. Add flour, whisk well.
  3. Microwave for 60 to 90 seconds. Center should be slightly molten. Be careful not to overcook!
  4. Enjoy with a spoon. Careful, the brownie will be hot.
