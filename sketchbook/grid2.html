Smorgasbord

Smorgasbord is a band of musicians from Huntsville, Alabama who enjoy making good sounds for the ear and soul. The brain-child of the brothers Paul and Will Cook, the band features different talented artists who like providing fun beats, tasty grooves, and memorable melodies for the people.
